About Rhyno EV

Our Mission

Established in 2019 by an automotive engineer with a vision for sustainable and robust mobility solutions, Rhyno EV isn't just a company; it's a collective effort of a dynamic team of young individuals passionately driving innovation in the electric vehicle industry. We believe in engineering solutions that solve problems.

Introducing the Future

With our first product, we've taken a bold step to create something more than just a vehicle; it's a unique experience of elegance, comfort, and style, addressing the pitfalls of conventional electric scooters. Our design prioritizes safety, eliminating concerns of fires and ensuring a longer battery lifespan with our innovative battery technology.

Built to Last, Built for You

  • Safety First: Design focused on fire prevention and enhanced battery life.
  • Fleet-Friendly: Rugged and reliable, ideal for tourist locations like Goa.
  • Aesthetic Appeal: Blending functionality with a sleek and stylish design.
